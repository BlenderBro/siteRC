Expect to be involved

In order to build your idea for it's proper target audience, we require all our clients
to be involved throughout the pre-development process.

Information Gathering

This first step is actually the most important one. It involves a solid understanding of the company it is created for. It involves a good understanding of you – what your business goals and dreams are, and how we can help you achieve these goals.

Planning

The end-user of your website or app – aka your customer – must be kept in mind during the entire planning phase. These are, after all, the people who will be learning about your service or buying your product.

Design

In this phase communication between both you and your designer is crucial. This stage is meant to ensure the final product will match your needs and taste. It’s very important to work closely with your designer, exchanging ideas, giving feedback and collaborating with each other until you both agree upon the final design of your website or app.

Tooling and resources

In the execution phase we will give your website a dedicated slot on our development server and assign you to a project manager. Depending on your time-to-market (the time you decide to go live), you will also have at least one full-time programmer and a designer. Throughout the execution, you will be able to check the progress of your website/ app using our milestone and issue tracker.
